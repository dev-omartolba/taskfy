## ✨ Why Taskfy?
- **AI Task Breakdown:** Automatically split complex goals into actionable subtasks.
- **Smart Scheduling:** AI-driven daily plans tailored to your energy levels.
- **Net Planner:** Map your tasks in a strategic network graph.
- **Gamified Progress:** Earn points, level up, and maintain streaks.
