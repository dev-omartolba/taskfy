# Taskfy: Level Up Your Life 🚀

**Taskfy** is more than just a to-do list; it is a comprehensive AI-powered life management ecosystem. It is designed to transform your most ambitious goals into small, actionable steps using advanced artificial intelligence, while making the process engaging and through deep gamification.

## 💡 The Concept
Taskfy bridges the gap between high-level strategic planning and daily execution. By leveraging cutting-edge AI features like **AI Task Breakdown** and the **Net Planner**, the app allows you to visualize the big picture of your projects and understand the critical dependencies between your tasks.

## 🌐 A True Cross-Platform Experience
Taskfy is built as a versatile "cross-over" system, ensuring your productivity follows you wherever you go:
- **Mobile:** A fast, interactive experience for productivity on the move.
- **Desktop:** Optimized for deep work and complex strategic planning (macOS / Windows).
- **Web & Extensions:** Integrated directly into your workflow (including specialized environments like VS Code).

## 🚀 Key Features
- **AI-Driven Strategy:** Turn dreams into structured action plans in seconds.
- **Gamified Quests:** A rewards-based task system that makes productivity feel like a game.
- **Strategic Mapping:** Visualize your progress in a network graph to understand the "Critical Path" of your goals.
- **Adaptive Scheduling:** An intelligent assistant that reshapes your day based on your real-time priorities and energy.
